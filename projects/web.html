Web Application Development

>Titan's Legacy

Titan's Legacy is a project I did with a few friends. We worked together over the course of 4 weeks to come up with an idea, design, and fully develop a web game. There were many difficulties along the way, and I learned a lot about Javascript and coding in a team environment throughout this project. It's hosted here, so check it out at https://titanslegacy.netlify.app/.

>Jurassic Park Website

At the Center for Advanced Research and Technology, I worked with a partner to design and build a website to model if Jurassic Park was a real company. Being my first big web project, I learned and grew a lot from problems with CSS animation, Javascript, and abstraction. Take a look around here https://jurassicpark-cart.netlify.app/.

>EECU Budget Calculator

EECU asked my peers and I to individually make digital budgeting calculators that follow their “Wise up” plan to educate youth how to deduct taxes and budget smartly. Over the course of a month, I gained experience as a project manager, overseeing my peers work on my website. Visit the budgeting tool at https://budgetcalculator-pod.netlify.app/.
